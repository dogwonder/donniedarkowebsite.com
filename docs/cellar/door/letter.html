10 . 02 . 1988
donnie —
the world did not end. you unmade it.
you carried the dark back through itself
and the count began again from nothing.
i wrote that every living creature on earth
dies alone. that was not the whole of it.
the dead wait behind a door you have
opened before. you will know the words
when you stand there — the most beautiful
in the language.
say them where the steps go down to the dark.
— r. sparrow
